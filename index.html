About Yves Group

Discretion is

Yves Group is a New York City–based private security and executive transportation firm serving high-profile individuals, corporate executives, and visiting dignitaries. We specialize in placing rigorously vetted executive protection agents and professional private drivers, providing seamless, low-profile coverage whether for a single evening, a week-long visit, or an ongoing protective arrangement.

Our team draws from backgrounds in law enforcement, military special operations, and private security — selected not only for their tactical expertise but for the composure and discretion demanded at the highest levels. Every detail is managed so our clients can move through the world with full confidence, without interruption to their lives or their privacy.
